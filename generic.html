Certificates

I am deeply honored to receive the Certificate of Appreciation for my role as a Digital geodata Volunteer during the 7-day Mapathon event organized by NAXA. This recognition underscores my commitment to leveraging geospatial technology for the greater good, particularly in disaster response and management. Contributing to the Development of Integrated Geodatabase project, I diligently digitized critical resources and identified open spaces, playing a vital role in enhancing digital geospatial data crucial for the project's success. I am grateful for the opportunity to contribute to such a meaningful initiative and extend my sincere appreciation to NAXA, the trainers, and all involved stakeholders for their support and collaboration.

I am thrilled to share my Certificate of Participation from the Geomatics Engineering Students' Association of Nepal (GESAN) for my active involvement in the Basic Arc GIS Training held from January 2 to 5, 2022. This esteemed recognition acknowledges my dedication to expanding my knowledge and skills in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) through hands-on learning and collaboration. I immersed myself in the fundamentals of ArcGIS, laying a solid foundation for my journey in geomatics engineering.

I am honored to receive the Certificate of Appreciation from the Geomatics Engineering Students' Association of Nepal, recognizing my valued contribution as a Member of Geo-World Volume-VI. This acknowledgment is a testament to my dedication and commitment to the field of geomatics engineering and highlights my active participation in advancing knowledge and collaboration within the community. I am grateful for the opportunity to contribute to Geo-World and to work alongside esteemed colleagues and mentors from Pashchimanchal Campus, Tribhuvan University.

I am delighted to receive the Certificate of Achievement from Tribhuvan University, Institute of Engineering, Pashchimanchal Campus, commemorating my success as the First Place Winner in the Geo-Quiz Contest held on the momentous occasion of the 35th Anniversary of the campus. This recognition is a testament to my dedication to academic excellence and my passion for Geomatics Engineering. I extend my heartfelt gratitude to my teammate, organizers, and fellow participants for providing me with this opportunity to showcase my skills and celebrate the rich legacy of Pashchimanchal Campus, Pokhara.

With great enthusiasm, I proudly showcase my Certificate of Participation awarded by the Geomatics Engineering Students' Association of Nepal for my attendance at the WebGIS Training. Collaboratively organized with OSGEO Nepal, this immersive program spanned from December 2023 to February 2024, encapsulating a transformative journey into the realm of geospatial technology. Throughout the training, I actively engaged in hands-on sessions, discussions, and collaborative projects, honing my skills in WebGIS techniques and methodologies.
