Investigating Earth's Circumference with Stellarium

I'm thrilled to share a project that delves into the versatile application of Stellarium, an innovative free and open-source planetarium software, in the realms of geodesy and astronomical observation. In this project, I explored the remarkable capabilities of Stellarium, renowned for its user-friendly interface and precise celestial simulations, which enable users to transform their home computers into virtual planetariums. Inspired by the groundbreaking experiment by Eratosthenes over two millennia ago, where he estimated Earth's circumference using the Sun's angle during the summer solstice at two locations, I sought to replicate his method virtually using Stellarium. This project not only showcases the power of modern technology in revisiting ancient scientific inquiries but also highlights the enduring significance of Eratosthenes' pioneering work in measuring our planet's size. By comparing our estimations with modern values, we shed light on the accuracy and relevance of Eratosthenes' method, underscoring the timeless impact of ancient scientific curiosity.

Unveiling Dynamic Changes in Butwal City's Landscape with Google Earth Engine

I'm thrilled to unveil my latest learning endeavor, focused on Land Use and Land Cover (LULC) classification in the vibrant city of Butwal, where I harnessed the powerful capabilities of Google Earth Engine (GEE). In this project, I embarked on a journey to analyze and visualize the dynamic changes in land utilization over the years 2018 and 2022, employing advanced remote sensing techniques and supervised classification algorithms. Using Sentinel-2 MSI data, I meticulously manipulated LULC classification data for Butwal Sub-metropolitan city, situated in the picturesque Rupandehi District. Leveraging the wide-swath, high-resolution imagery provided by Sentinel-2, I delved into the intricate details of Butwal's landscape, monitoring changes in vegetation, soil, water cover, and more. One of the highlights of this project was the utilization of the Random Forest (RF) supervised machine learning classifier, renowned for its exceptional classification accuracy and processing speed. By selecting and preparing train and test datasets encompassing all classes of interest, I trained the classifier to accurately classify LULC maps for 2018 and 2022.

Harnessing Machine Learning and Remote Sensing for Forest Fire Prediction and Assessment

I'm currently immersed in a groundbreaking project focused on forest fire prediction and assessment, leveraging the powerful combination of machine learning and remote sensing technologies. By analyzing historical data and remote sensing imagery, I'm working towards developing a predictive model to assess the likelihood and severity of forest fires in specific regions. Through the integration of advanced machine learning algorithms, I aim to identify key factors contributing to forest fires, such as weather patterns, vegetation density, and human activities. By harnessing the predictive capabilities of these algorithms and the suitable probablity theorem, we can anticipate potential fire hotspots and take proactive measures to mitigate the risk of catastrophic events. While the project is still in progress, I'm excited about the potential impact it holds in safeguarding our precious natural resources and mitigating the devastating effects of forest fires.

Introducing an Interactive Web Map for Exploring Colleges in Pokhara Municipality!

Excited to unveil my latest project- an innovative web map developed using Leaflet.js and deployed through GitHub. This dynamic tool empowers individuals to seamlessly explore undergraduate and postgraduate colleges nestled within the core area of Pokhara Municipality, Nepal. With the click of a button, users can effortlessly locate colleges and gain valuable insights into the distribution of educational institutions across the region. Through proportional and choropleth mapping techniques, the web map visualizes the number of students enrolled in each college, providing a comprehensive overview of educational demographics in Pokhara. But the features don't stop there! Leveraging advanced routing functionalities, the web map also offers users the convenience of accessing detailed directions to their desired colleges from their current locations. Whether you're a prospective student or a curious explorer, this interactive tool promises an enriching experience tailored to your needs. As we strive to promote access to education and foster informed decision-making, I'm proud to contribute to the development of this impactful resource.
